goal

We have pledged to donate half the profits from t-shirt sales to help protect UK waterways. This page exists as a public record of what comes in, what is spent, what remains as profit, and what is given away.

It also exists to make visible the economics around small-scale art production: what things cost, what artists are left with, and where money actually goes.

how it works

A t-shirt selling for £30 usually returns around £10 profit after production and platform costs. Of that £10, we pledge to donate £5 towards protecting UK waterways.

Figures may change slightly depending on print costs, payment processing fees, discounts, and shipping structures. This page will be updated to reflect the real numbers.
